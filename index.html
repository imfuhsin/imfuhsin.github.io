Logo Design

Taiwan Visual

Taiwan visual is a knowledge-sharing platform for designers. It provides news and porfolio of design and visual, and also be awebsite for job seeking. "Visual" sounds like "rice wine" in chinese, therefore, the logo shaped like a wine bottle and colored with vivid green presents young enregy in Taiwan design.

Career Meet

Career Meet is a website and app for Career experience exchange. Besides the discussions of careers and company, there will be a quick process for face-to-face meeting booking. The concept of logo is from the finger print, which means an agrrement and commitment.

Phoday

Photoday is a website for photography appointment. People can find their favorite photography style on the website and book for a photography service. Photographer can post their works and held photography activity through Photoday. The slogan for photoday is "Booking series of photos today".

Prototype of Website

This is a quick prototype for photographer page on Photoday. There is a jumbotron to show their works. Then there are a quick instruction and quote from the photographer. Users can choose their favorite photography style and book for the service. It also shows price in image card and comments from customers at bottom.

Workinterest is a platform for job seeking. User can share news and follow persons and companies from the fields they interested in. Workinterest also provides job seekers a intellegent system to find the suitable jobs matched by skills and interested fields.
